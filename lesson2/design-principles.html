Alignment

Company: ESPN

https://espn.com

ESPN does a great job of modeling proper alignment along theier horizontal planes. Notice how all the elements allow for easy reading and flow. Your eye isn't distracted by chaos, it is all very natural.

Rule of Thirds

Company: Arizona Sports

https://arizonasports.com

The image of the Suns owner, Rober Sarver, in the image here is a good example of the rule of thirds. You can see that by framing the shot with Robert off to the side, it provides a pleasant viewing experience and invites the reader to continue reading.

White Space

Company: Google

https://google.com

Google is the classic example of the proper use of white space. They have a simple site, and they treat it properly by not overwhelming us with clutter or too many options that are mushed together. This allows for a great user experience.

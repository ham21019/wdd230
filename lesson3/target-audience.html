Target Audience:

The Queen Creek Chamber of Commerce serves the local community in and around the Town of Queen Creek, AZ. The town has experienced rapid growth over the last decade and is currently experiencing a boom of new residents, restaurants, and small business growth. Many new residents don't know what services are available and don't know what they are missing out on. Many local businesses would like to have better opportunities but haven't figured out how to market to their immediate neighbors. This site is designed to help both local patrons and businesses find each other and create successful relationships.

Target Personas:

Mary Jackson

Mary owns a small furniture company. She is married with 2 kids. She would like to connect with more local customers. She offers free delivery to local residents.

Tom Dixon

Tom has just moved to Queen Creek, AZ. He would like to know what services are available in town. If coupons or other discounts are available, he would give preference to these shops.

Listed Scenarios:

  • I am interesting in locally owned restaurants, not chain stores. Do you have a list?
  • My business would like to advertise to just town members. How do I do that?
  • I would like a permit to open my own business. Where do I go to file the paperwork?
  • Are there any groups that meet on a weekly or monthly basis for XYZABC activity?
  • Do you have any vegan or gluten free dessert restaurants in the area?

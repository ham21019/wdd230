About Us

Bountiful Foods was founded in 2017 to serve the beach going public of Southern California.

We were born out of a desire to serve freshly sourced organic produce when you need to cool off the most, at the beach!

No longer do you have to shower, get changed, dust all the sand out of your car, and then try to figure out where to get cool refreshment.

We bring "the cool" to you!
